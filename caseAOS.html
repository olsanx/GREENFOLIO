Case Study

AOS Projects Management Limited

CONSTRUCTION BRAND SITE

A growing construction firm had the capability, but nothing online to prove it. Built from scratch to establish credibility, showcase work, and make contact frictionless.

Role: Design & Development · Year: 2025

Live Site GitHub

Overview

This project involved building a website for a growing construction company that previously had no online presence. The goal was to create a platform that builds credibility, showcases completed work, and allows clients to easily get in touch.

Problem

  • No website or online presence
  • No structured project showcase
  • No easy way for clients to reach out

Solution

  • Built a responsive and functional website
  • Clear project showcase with consistent visual hierarchy
  • Integrated live chat for instant communication

Process

Impact

AOS Projects now has a digital presence that holds its own against larger, better-funded competitors. A first-time visitor understands the offer within seconds and has a direct, frictionless path to get in touch, whether on desktop or mobile.
