Case Study

Nola Energy

BUSINESS LANDING PAGE

Built to communicate the company’s offer within seconds, this landing page focuses on clarity, structure, and search visibility to strengthen Nola Energy’s online presence.

Role: Development & SEO Structuring · Year: 2025

Live Site GitHub

Overview

This project involved designing and developing a landing page for Nola Energy, with a focus on presenting their services clearly and improving their visibility on search engines. The goal was to create a fast, structured experience that helps visitors understand the offer immediately.

Problem

  • No clear online presentation of services
  • Low visibility on search engines
  • Lack of structure for guiding user attention

Solution

  • Developed a structured, responsive landing page
  • Used semantic HTML to improve SEO and indexing
  • Applied clear hierarchy to communicate the offer quickly

Process

01

02

Planned layout for fast comprehension

03

04

Optimized structure for search engine visibility

Impact

The final result is a clear and accessible landing page that communicates Nola Energy’s services within seconds. The improved structure supports better search visibility while giving potential clients a direct and confident understanding of the business.
