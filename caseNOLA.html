This project involved designing and developing a landing page for Nola Energy, with a focus on presenting their services clearly and improving their visibility on search engines.

The goal was to create a fast, structured experience that helps visitors understand the offer immediately while supporting long-term discoverability.

  • –Lack of structure for guiding user attention
Solution
  • →Developed a structured, responsive landing page

  • →Applied clear hierarchy for fast comprehension
